Project Title: Job Portal - GROW

Short Summany

A dynamic job portal that helps seekers find relevant opportunities for growth while connecting companies with diligent and skilled talent.

Key Features
  • Autocomplete enabled search option for jobs and candidates(based of skills).
  • Advanced filters like location, type and category.
  • Stripe integration for payment plans.
  • Google charts dashboard for admin insights.
  • Email option using Django mail.
  • User-Friendly interface.
  • Hashed passwords for security.
  • OTP Verification (Sent through mail).
